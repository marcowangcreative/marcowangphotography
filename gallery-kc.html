Getting Ready at The Houstonian Hotel Houston

The morning of February 15th inside The Houstonian’s suites - the same quiet laughter that carried them through a dead phone and a downpour at the Houston Zoo, now set against martini glasses and the careful hands that make everything beautiful.

Wedding Ceremony at The Houstonian Hotel Houston

Kirby and Chase Couple Portraits at The Houstonian Hotel

“Chase proposed at the same reflection pool in Hermann Park where they got caught in the rain on their first date. Right where it all began.”

Their wedding day became a local holiday. The Mayor of Houston declared it “Kirby and Chase Day.” Valentine’s weekend, candlelit tables beneath The Houstonian’s grand ceilings, and hundreds of red roses hanging from above.

Luxury Wedding Reception at The Houstonian Hotel Houston

Then the bride changed into red sequins, kicked off her heels, and surprised the groom with a DJ set that kept the dance floor packed until last call.

Heart-shaped sunglasses, a teddy bear in a tuxedo, and the bride behind the decks — this was the kind of night no one wanted to end.
