About Marco

Marco Wang didn’t start behind a screen. He started in a darkroom, breathing chemicals and ruining prints until they finally worked. That’s probably why his photos from twenty years ago don’t look dated. He never chased trends, so there’s nothing to age out.

He’s based in Houston and keeps coming back to it, even after shooting across the West Coast and Asia. Hundreds of weddings in backyards, ballrooms, everything in between, and the job still comes down to the same thing: pay attention or miss it. Most people miss it.

He’s a father, which means the father-daughter dance isn’t just part of the timeline. It’s the part where things get real, whether anyone admits it or not.

His work has shown up in The New York Times, Style Me Pretty, and Modern Luxury. Which is nice. But the real job is still the same: don’t screw up the moment you can’t redo.

Jordan Wang

Over a decade immersed in styling, driven by a genuine love for beauty in all its forms. Hair, texture, detail, and the way everything comes together. Based in Houston, with work that travels, the focus is always the same: helping people feel like themselves, just elevated.

Every Marco Wang Photography booking includes a styling consultation with Jordan. She works closely with each client to shape the overall look, from hair to feel to intention, so nothing is left to chance and everything feels cohesive before the day even begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your wedding photography style?

Marco Wang Photography specializes in fine art, editorial wedding photography with a timeless, photojournalistic approach. Based in Houston, Texas, Marco blends candid documentary moments with refined, magazine-quality portraits. His style is intentionally classic, designed to look as beautiful in twenty years as it does today.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes. While based in Houston, Texas, Marco Wang photographs destination weddings worldwide. Popular destinations include Tuscany, Rome, Lake Como, the Amalfi Coast, Paris, the South of France, Provence, Santorini, the Greek Islands, London, the Scottish Highlands, Tulum, Los Cabos, Napa Valley, Aspen, and New York City. Recent work includes Ombroneta Villa in Tuscany and Château de Farcheville near Paris. Travel fees vary by location.

What areas do you serve in Texas?

Marco Wang Photography is based in Houston, Texas and serves the greater Houston area, including The Woodlands, Sugar Land, Galveston, and Austin. He also regularly photographs weddings in Dallas, San Antonio, and throughout Texas.

How far in advance should I book a wedding photographer?

We recommend booking 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October through May in Houston) and destination weddings. Marco Wang Photography is currently accepting bookings for 2026 and 2027.

Do you offer engagement sessions?

Yes. Engagement sessions are available as standalone sessions or as part of a wedding photography package. Recent engagement sessions include New York City and Aspen, Colorado. Sessions can take place in Houston or at a destination of your choice.

What publications have featured your work?

Marco Wang's wedding photography has been featured in The New York Times, Inside Weddings, and Modern Luxury Weddings, among other publications.

What venues have you photographed weddings at in Houston?

Marco has photographed weddings at many of Houston's top venues including The Houstonian Hotel, Hotel Granduca, The Corinthian, The Bell Tower on 34th, River Oaks Country Club, Houston Country Club, Lakeside Country Club, Post Oak Hotel, The Thompson Hotel, and many private estates throughout the Houston area.
